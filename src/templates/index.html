What is Excess Insurance?

Car hire companies often charge an excess for damage caused to the car whilst you're behind the wheel even if it's not your fault. This can be upwards of £1000, meaning that if you're involved in an accident, you'll have to pay a contribution to cover the damage.

With an annual car hire insurance policy from insurance4carhire, you're able to reclaim the excess you’d have to pay in the unfortunate event of an accident or your car being stolen.

What do we cover?

  • Drivers between the ages of 18 and 85
  • Drivers who are residents of*:
    France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, Spain, Sweden, UK
  • Trips up to 60 days in-a-row
  • As well as excess cover, we also offer Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) and Supplemental Liability Insurance (SLI). Altough many car rental companies automatically include these covers in their car rental insurance, some don’t - so it’s important to chek the terms of your car rental agreement.
